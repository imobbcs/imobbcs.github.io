Imo Babics is a prominent fintech executive who serves as the Chief Growth Officer and CMO at Relai, Europe's leading MiCA-licensed Bitcoin app. Originally from Slovakia, he moved to the UK to study Business Management at the University of Surrey, and spent his formative years building a career in London, before eventually making Vienna his home.

He has 17 years in marketing and has been part of the Bitcoin industry since 2017. Previously, he was Head of Marketing at Bitpanda, where he scaled the platform from 100,000 to over 1 million users and built the marketing team from zero to seventeen people. Before fintech he spent a decade in media and entertainment, working with Universal Pictures, Xbox and PlayStation. In 2026, he was named one of the Fintech Marketing Hub's Top 30 Most Influential Fintech Marketers in Europe.

As Chief Growth Officer, Chief Marketing Officer, and Managing Director of the EU entity, Imo is a core member of Relai's leadership team. Beyond steering the company's commercial trajectory, he co-runs the overarching business alongside his C-suite peers, shaping corporate strategy and making critical operational decisions across all facets of the organisation.

On the execution side, Imo has full ownership of how the company grows, competes, and is perceived. He sets the brand and growth strategy, owns performance and analytics across all acquisition channels, leads Relai Private & Business, and runs the Growth department spanning marketing, content, social, PR, and customer experience. Under his leadership, Relai has grown to over 600,000 app downloads and $1 billion in total volume, expanded across Germany, Italy, Spain and beyond, and navigated MiCA compliance without sacrificing growth velocity.

Imo is available for conference talks, podcast appearances, and media requests on Bitcoin adoption and startup growth in Europe. He is also open to board and non-executive director conversations with Bitcoin or fintech companies operating in Europe. Get in touch at hello [at] imobabics.com or via LinkedIn.
