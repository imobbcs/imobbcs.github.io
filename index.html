About

I've done this before. Twice.

I scaled Bitpanda from 100,000 to over 1 million users. Built the marketing team from scratch — zero to seventeen people — and owned acquisition, retention, and brand across Europe. Then left.

FAQ

Common questions

The things people ask before they get in touch.

  • Imo Babics is the Chief Growth Officer and CMO at Relai, Europe's leading MiCA-licensed Bitcoin app. He has 17 years in marketing and over 8 years in Bitcoin. Previously, he was Head of Marketing at Bitpanda, where he scaled the platform from 100,000 to over 1 million users and built the marketing team from zero to seventeen people.
  • At Relai, Imo leads all growth and marketing as CGO & CMO, and serves as Managing Director of Relai's EU entity. He has helped grow the platform to over 600,000 app downloads and $1 billion in total volume, expanded across Germany, Italy, Spain and other EU markets, and navigated MiCA compliance without sacrificing growth velocity.
  • MiCA (Markets in Crypto-Assets Regulation) is Europe's regulatory framework for crypto-asset service providers. Rather than being a barrier, MiCA effectively unlocks access to 450 million potential users across the EU under a single licence. Most operators treat it as a compliance burden — the contrarian view is that it's Bitcoin's biggest tailwind in Europe when used correctly.
  • The core insight is that most growth problems aren't growth problems — they're clarity problems. If a company can't explain what it does in one sentence, no amount of spend will fix it. In regulated markets specifically, growth is cross-functional: marketing, product, tech, compliance, and support all shape outcomes. Regulation handled well becomes a distribution advantage, not a constraint.
