About

I've done this before. Twice.

Chief Growth Officer and CMO at Relai — a MiCA-licensed, Bitcoin-only self-custody app operating across DACH and the rest of Europe.

I scaled Bitpanda from 100,000 to over 1 million users. Built the marketing team from scratch — zero to seventeen people — and owned acquisition, retention, and brand across Europe. Then left.

At Relai, I joined early to help anchor its strategic direction. The job is to turn Bitcoin into a trusted savings product for Europeans — across brand, performance, product growth, lifecycle, and private and business segments. As a member of the C-suite, I actively co-steer the corporate strategy, product lifecycle, and cross-functional operations that shape where the company is going, not just how we market it.

Before fintech: a decade in media and entertainment, working with Universal Pictures, Xbox, PlayStation, Microsoft, and Huawei.
